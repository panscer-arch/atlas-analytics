Smart Cycle

Provide help. Complete the cycle. Request help back.

Smart Cycle-1 is the first core product of Atlas. The pre-launch page only gives a short explanation, while deeper mechanics stay in separate educational videos and docs.

Requests and calculated deltas depend on smart-contract rules, community activity and available liquidity. They should be studied as risk-bearing participation, not as a guaranteed income product.
